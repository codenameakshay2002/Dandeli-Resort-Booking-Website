Dandeli Resorts

Dandeli, located in Karnataka, is a picturesque paradise known for its lush green forests, pristine streams, and rich flora and fauna. The region has gained popularity for its wildlife sanctuaries and adventure sports offerings, attracting tourists from India and abroad. Dandeli is the second largest wildlife sanctuary in Karnataka and was declared a tiger reserve in 20074 . The area boasts a variety of resorts that cater to different preferences and offer unique experiences.
